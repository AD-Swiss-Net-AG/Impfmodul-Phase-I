Adverse event

Detailed view of a selected adverse event or unwanted immunization event (UIE) from the patient's EPD.

The detail view displays the technical data of the adverse event. The detail view serves as a starting point for editing and commenting, for cancellation, or for downloading an selected adverse reaction and supports interactions:

  • Switch to the dialog for editing the data of the selected adverse reaction
  • Switch to the dialog to annul side effects entered by mistake
  • Download of the data set according to EPD exchange format as JSON.

The detail view shows the following technical details:

  • Date: Date of occurrence of the adverse reaction
  • Adverse effect: The name of the unwanted side effect
  • Recorded by: The name of the person who recorded the adverse reaction information
  • Organization: name of the organization where the vaccination was administered
