Risk factors

List of all data records stored in the EPD on a patient's risk factors. Data records that have been corrected, commented on, or annulled by other data records are also displayed. and are therefore not displayed in the immunization record.

This display is used to review and correct inconsistencies in the immunization record and need only be used to Detect and clean up the inconsistencies. The display supports the interactions for this purpose:

  • Switching to the detailed view from an risk factor by clicking on an entry
  • Add new entries of risk factors

The display shows the list of all documented basic diseases sorted by date of occurrence and with the following fields:

  • Date: Date of occurrence of the risk factor
  • Disease: The name of the risk factor
  • Clinical status: The clinical condition, distinguishing between active or inactive
  • Recorded by: Name of the person who made the diagnosis.
