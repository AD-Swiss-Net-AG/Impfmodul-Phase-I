Adverse effects

.

List of all data records stored in the EPD on the side effects or unwanted immunization events (UIE) of a patient. The list also includes data records that have been corrected, commented on or annulled by other data records. and therefore are not displayed in the immunization record.

This display is used to check for and correct inconsistencies in the immunization record and only needs to be used in order to and clean them up. The representation supports the interactions for this purpose:

  • Switch to the detailed view of an adverse event by clicking on an entry
  • Adding a new adverse reaction

The display shows the list of all documented side effects sorted by date of occurrence and with the following fields:

  • Date: Date of occurrence of the adverse reaction
  • Adverse effect: The name of the adverse reaction
  • Recorded by: Name of the person who recorded the adverse reaction information
