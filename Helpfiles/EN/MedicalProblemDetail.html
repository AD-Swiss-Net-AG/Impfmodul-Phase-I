Medical problems

Detail view of a selected medical problem from the EPD of the patient.

The detail view displays the professional data of a selected medical problem. The detail view serves as a starting point for editing and commenting, for cancellation or for downloading an selected vaccination and supports interactions:

  • Switch to the dialog for editing the data of the selected medical problem
  • Switch to the dialog for the annulation of fälsch entered medical problems
  • Download of the data set according to EPD exchange format as JSON.

The detail view shows the following technical details:

  • Date: Date of diagnosis of the medical problem
  • Medical problem: Name of the respective medical problem
  • Start: Date of onset of symptoms of the medical problem
  • End: Date of recovery
  • Clinical status: The clinical state in which a distinction is made between active or inactive
  • Collected by: Name of the person who made the diagnosis.
  • Organization: Name of the organization where the vaccination was performed.
