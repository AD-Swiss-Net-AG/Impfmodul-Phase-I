Infectious diseases

.

List of all data records stored in the EPD on vaccine-preventable infectious diseases of a patient. Also displayed are records that have been corrected, annotated, or cancelled by other records and are therefore not in the vaccination record. and therefore are not displayed in the immunization record.

This display is used to check for and correct inconsistencies in the immunization record and only needs to be used in order to Identify and clean up the inconsistencies. The representation supports the interactions for this purpose:
  • Switch to the detailed view from a vaccine-preventable disease by clicking on an entry
  • Add new entries of vaccine-preventable diseases

In the display, the list of all documented vaccine-preventable infectious diseases is sorted by date of Occurrence and displayed with the following fields:

  • Date: Date of diagnosis of infectious disease
  • Infectious disease: The name of the infectious disease
  • Recorded by: Name of the person who made the infectious disease diagnosis
