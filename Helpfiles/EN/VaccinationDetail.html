Vaccinations

Detail view of a selected vaccination from the patient's EPD.

The detail view displays the technical data of a selected vaccination. The detail view serves as a starting point for editing and commenting, for cancellation or for downloading an selected vaccination and supports interactions:

  • Switch to the dialog for editing the data of the selected vaccination
  • Switch to the dialog for cancelling vaccinations entered by mistake
  • Download of the data set according to EPD exchange format as JSON.

The detail view shows the following technical details:

  • Date: Date of vaccination
  • Vaccine: The vaccine administered
  • Vaccination protection: One or more infectious diseases against which the vaccine is effective
  • Dose: Number of vaccine dose administered, xth dose
  • Lot number: The lot number of the vaccine
  • Reason for vaccination: Reason why vaccination was given
  • Vaccinated By: Name of the health professional who administered the vaccination
  • Organization: Name of the organization where the vaccination was administered
  • Comments: All comments on the selected entry, per entry name of the creator and the date and content is displayed.
