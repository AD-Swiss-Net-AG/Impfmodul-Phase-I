Vaccination record

Total representation of immunization data from a patient's EPD. To display the immunization record. all vaccination documents from the patient's EPD are retrieved from the EPD and displayed according to the rule set for EPD immunization data are summarized.

The immunization record serves as the starting point for processing patient immunization data in EPD and supports interactions:

  • Switch to detailed view of vaccinations, adverse events, infectious diseases and risk factors by clicking on an entry
  • Export the immunization record as a PDF document
  • Save the vaccination card as a snapshot at the present time in VACD exchange format.

The immunization card will display the following immunization data:

Vaccinations

List of all documented vaccinations sorted by date of vaccination with fields:

  • Date: Date of vaccination
  • Vaccination protection: One or more infectious diseases against which the vaccine is effective
  • Vaccine: The vaccine administered
  • Dose: Number of the vaccine dose administered
  • Vaccinated By: Name of the health professional who administered the vaccination or if not known, name of the organization where the vaccination was administered.

Adverse effects

List of all documented adverse effect symptoms, ordered by date of occurrence with the fields:

  • Date: Date of occurrence of the adverse effect certificate
  • Adverse effect: The name of the adverse effect
  • Recoreded by: Name of the person who noted the adverse reaction

Infectious diseases

List of all vaccine-preventable diseases the patient has been through, sorted by diagnosis date with the fields:

  • Date: Date of diagnosis of the vaccine-preventable disease passed
  • Infectious disease: Name of the respective vaccine-preventable disease
  • Recorded by: Name of the person who made the infectious disease diagnosis

Risk factors

List of risk factors of the patient, ordered by diagnosis date with the fields:

  • Date: Date of diagnosis of risk factor
  • Basic disease: Name of the respective risk factor
  • Clinical status: The clinical condition, distinguishing between active or inactive
  • Recorded by: Name of the person who made the diagnosis for the risk factor
