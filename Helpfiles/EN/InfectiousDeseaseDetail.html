Infectious diseases

Detail view of a selected vaccine-preventable disease from the patient's EPD.

The detailed view displays the technical data of a selected vaccine-preventable disease. The detail view serves as a starting point for editing and commenting, canceling, or downloading a selected vaccination and supports interactions:

  • Switch to the dialog for editing the data of the selected infectious disease
  • Switch to the dialog for annulation of infectious disease entered by mistake
  • Download of the data set according to EPD exchange format as JSON.

The detail view shows the following technical details:

  • Date: Date of diagnosis of the vaccine-preventable disease undergone
  • Infectious disease: Name of the respective vaccine-preventable disease
  • Begin: Date of onset of symptoms of the vaccine-preventable disease
  • End: Date of recovery
  • Recorded by: Name of person who made the diagnosis
  • Organization: Name of the organization where the vaccination was administered
