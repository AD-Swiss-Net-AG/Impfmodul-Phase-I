Vaccinations

.

List of all vaccination records stored in the patient's EPD. Also displayed are records that have been corrected, commented on, or cancelled by other records and are therefore not displayed in the vaccination record. are not displayed.

This display is used to check for and correct inconsistencies in the immunization record and need only be used to Identify and clean up inconsistencies. The representation supports the interactions for this purpose:

  • Switch to the detailed view of immunizations by clicking on an entry
  • Adding a new vaccination entry

The display shows the list of all documented vaccinations sorted by date of vaccination and with the following fields displayed:

  • Date: Date of vaccination
  • Vaccination protection: One or more infectious diseases against which the vaccine is effective
  • Vaccine: The vaccine administered
  • Dose: Number of the vaccine dose administered
  • Vaccinated By: Name of the health professional who administered the vaccination or if not known, name of the organization where the vaccination was administered.
